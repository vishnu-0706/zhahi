One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese anime and manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. The story follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate who dreams of becoming the King of the Pirates.

Plot

The story is set in a world dominated by oceans, where pirates roam freely. Luffy gains the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit.

His goal is to find the legendary treasure called One Piece, which was left behind by the former Pirate King, GDR.

“Wealth, fame, power… Gold Roger, the King of the Pirates, attained this and everything else the world had to offer.”
